How do I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation online through our website, by calling our customer service at (800) 123-4567, or by visiting one of our locations in person. For online reservations, simply navigate to the "Cars" page, select your desired vehicle, choose your rental dates, and complete the booking process.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

To rent a car, you will need:

  • A valid driver's license (held for at least 1 year)
  • A major credit card in your name
  • Proof of insurance
  • A valid ID or passport (for non-US residents)

For luxury or high-end vehicles, additional documentation or higher age requirements may apply.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service. Modifications and cancellations made at least 48 hours before the scheduled pickup time are free of charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee of up to one day's rental cost.

What is included in the rental price?

Our standard rental price includes:

  • The vehicle rental for the specified period
  • Standard liability insurance
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • Unlimited mileage (for most vehicles)

Additional services such as premium insurance coverage, GPS navigation, child seats, and additional drivers are available for an extra fee.

Are there any hidden fees?

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. All mandatory fees are included in your quoted price. Additional fees may apply for:

  • Late returns (charged hourly after a 30-minute grace period)
  • Fuel replacement (if the vehicle is not returned with the same fuel level)
  • Excessive cleaning (if the vehicle requires special cleaning)
  • Traffic or parking violations
  • Damage repairs not covered by insurance

All potential fees are clearly outlined in your rental agreement.

What types of vehicles do you offer?

Our premium fleet includes a wide range of vehicles to suit your needs:

  • Luxury Sedans (Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi)
  • Sports Cars (Porsche, Maserati, Jaguar)
  • SUVs (Range Rover, Cadillac Escalade, BMW X7)
  • Convertibles (Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi)
  • Executive Vans (Mercedes-Benz V-Class)
  • Electric Vehicles (Tesla, Audi e-tron)

Visit our "Cars" page to see our complete selection with detailed specifications and availability.

Can I request a specific vehicle model?

Yes, at Premium Car Rental, we offer model-specific reservations. When you book with us, you're guaranteed the exact make and model of the vehicle you select, not just a vehicle class. This ensures you get precisely the driving experience you desire.

What insurance options do you offer?

We offer several insurance options to protect you during your rental:

  • Standard Coverage (included): Provides basic liability protection
  • Premium Coverage: Reduces your deductible and adds comprehensive protection
  • Full Coverage: Eliminates your deductible entirely and provides maximum protection
  • Personal Effects Coverage: Protects your belongings inside the vehicle

Our staff can help you select the best coverage for your needs at pickup.

What is your fuel policy?

We operate on a "full-to-full" fuel policy. This means your vehicle will be provided with a full tank of fuel, and you're expected to return it with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned with less fuel than at pickup, a refueling charge will apply, which includes the cost of the missing fuel plus a service fee.

What is your age requirement for renting?

Our standard minimum age requirement is 25 years for most vehicles. Drivers between 21-24 years may be eligible to rent certain vehicle categories with an additional young driver surcharge. For high-performance and luxury vehicles, the minimum age requirement is 30 years. All drivers must have held a valid driver's license for at least one year.

Can I take the rental car across state lines or into another country?

Travel within the contiguous United States is generally permitted with our vehicles. However, if you plan to travel to certain states or into Canada or Mexico, you must inform us at the time of reservation. Additional fees and special insurance may be required for international travel. Some high-end vehicles may have geographical restrictions due to insurance and security considerations.
